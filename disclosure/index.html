Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 2026

We believe in being straight with you about how Love Your Senses makes money, so here it is in plain terms.

What affiliate links mean

Some of the links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and go on to buy something, we may earn a small commission from the retailer. It never costs you anything extra, and it does not change the price you pay.

We take part in affiliate programmes including CJ, also known as Commission Junction, and Awin, among others. These connect us with fragrance and lifestyle retailers whose products we recommend.

Our promise

The products and scent families our quizzes recommend are chosen on merit. Your result is based on your answers, not on which retailer pays us. A commission never decides what we suggest to you.

These small commissions are simply what keep our quizzes free to take, and let us keep building new ones.

If anything here is unclear, or you would like to know more, please get in touch at hello@loveyoursenses.com.
